Finance Intern

Overview

  • Our Finance Internship program offers students and recent graduates an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience in the field of finance. Interns will work closely with our finance team on various projects, including financial analysis, budgeting, and reporting. This internship is ideal for individuals looking to develop essential finance skills, gain exposure to real-world financial operations, and prepare for a career in finance. As a Finance Intern at Ventus Pay, participants will assist in various financial processes and gain exposure to different facets of corporate finance. They will be involved in tasks such as financial modeling, data analysis, and assisting with financial audits, all of which are crucial for understanding the dynamics of the finance sector. This role provides a solid foundation for those aspiring to build a successful career in finance by fostering both professional skills and personal growth.

    • Responsibilities

    1. Assist in preparing financial reports, forecasts, and budgets.
    2. Conduct data analysis to support financial decision-making.
    3. Support the finance team in tracking expenses and analyzing variances.
    4. Help in maintaining financial records and ensuring accuracy in all financial documents.
    5. Participate in financial audits and help ensure compliance with regulations.
    6. Contribute to the preparation of presentations for internal and external stakeholders.
    7. Collaborate with various departments to gather necessary financial data.
    8. Assist with cash flow analysis and monitoring of financial performance indicators.
    9. Participate in training sessions to learn about financial systems and tools used within the company.
    10. Support other finance-related tasks as assigned by the team.
  • This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties, or responsibilities required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time with or without notice.

    • Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent: Possession of a high school diploma or equivalent educational qualification.

    • Job-related experience

    1. Strong interest in finance and a desire to learn about corporate finance operations.
    2. Basic knowledge of financial principles and accounting.
    3. Proficient computer skills, including familiarity with Microsoft Excel (will provide training).
    4. Excellent analytical and numerical skills.
    5. Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
    6. Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment.
    7. Applicants must be at least 18 years old and pursuing a degree in finance, accounting, business, or a related field.
    8. Ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise discretion with sensitive information.

    Job-Type

    1. Remote

    Job-related skills/competencies

    1. Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
    2. Proficiency in MS-Excel, PowerPoint, and Word.
    3. Effective communication and writing skills.
    4. Strong time management skills and ability to meet deadlines.
